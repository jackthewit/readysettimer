      A timer built for speakers

      ReadySetTimer is a free online presentation & speech timer. Stage-visible tabular digits, a background that shifts color as time runs out, a big 6-second final countdown, and a one-key fullscreen mode that hides all UI chrome. Pomodoro and a CSV-exporting stopwatch are built in.

      Use it for conference talks, lightning talks, pitches, webinars, MC duties, debates, interview rehearsals, or everyday focus blocks. No sign-up, no install, no tracking — your settings live only in your browser.

      Why ReadySetTimer

      • Stage-visible digits with circular progress ring
      • Color shift — cyan → amber → red as time runs down
      • Final 6 seconds — a screen-filling number replaces the UI
      • Fullscreen mode with a single F key press
      • Pomodoro — 25/5/15 auto-cycle, fully customizable
      • Stopwatch — per-lap memos, CSV export (UTF-8 BOM)
      • 8 languages — Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German
